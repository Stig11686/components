Small Kitchens

Some apartments and homes can lack in kitchen space, luckily, there are many kitchen ideas that can maximize storage and efficiency. When you design your kitchen you need to ensure you work to the layout of your space, whether your kitchen is confined to a single wall, U-shaped, or tucked in a corner.

Have a think about the below ideas:

  • Pendant lights / striking light fixtures
  • Lighting can brighten up your entire kitchen  

You can never have enough lighting in a kitchen!

  • Being organised

Being organised is a must in a small kitchen, when space is at a premium being organised inside your cabinets is needed, use storage trays, boxes to keep everything tidy and keeping all your worktops minimalist as this can enhance space making it feel larger

  • Painting & Tiling

Using lighter colours can help visualise expansion! Even choosing lighter worktops will help with that space feel, making it feel more open.
